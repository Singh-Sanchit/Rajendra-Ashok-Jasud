aboutabout myself

I, a man widely recognized not only for his exceptional business acumen but also for my vision of a bright and secure future for the underprivileged citizens of India. I comes from a very humble background. I was born in the Shirur district of Pune, Maharashtra. I was able to achieve the pinnacle of success through constant perseverance and hard work and in this journey I have been the source of inspiration to millions.

Successful Businessman and a Philanthropist needs to be discussed simultaneously in order to throw light on my dynamism and goodwill. My journey begin from being a plastic scrap dealer to currently being the owner of Manbras Plastronics Pvt.Ltd. is one of severe hardships but sheer excellence. The company is one of the few companies in Maharashtra dealing in plastic recycling. Such noble venture separates him from other businessmen of the country. I also ventured into the logistic business in the United States of America (USA) and further has Import-Export business with the United Arab Emirates (UAE).

I have a heart that resonates with the poor and underprivileged of India has started his groundwork in tune with his vision for better quality and holistic education, better health and hygiene through blood and food donation camps, building hospitals and nursing homes equipped with advanced medical facilities. With my hardwork and financial contribution has helped to build more than 8 schools in different parts of Maharashtra and is also helping several other schools with their repairs and maintenance. It is worth mentioning some of my work in transforming the lives of school students through generosity and charity. 700 students of Bhairavnath Vidhyalay in Karde, 30 students in Anganwadi , Talvadi, 25 students of JP Prathamik Shala, Langevadi and 39 students of JP Prathamik Shala in Chavan Wadi are now benefitting from my work.

My first brain child, an NGO named Shri Bhairavnath Lokseva Gramvikas Sanstha is engaged in providing safe and clean living conditions, a better standard of living and a platform of opportunities and growth for those in need. My work does not end here as I continues to endeavour to start more such NGOs for the poor, downtrodden and weaker socio-economic sections of our society.

The sole mission is to reduce and eradicate the sufferings and hardships of the poor. My life motto is based on his own background "Not to let people suffer the way I faced suffering in my life".

I am not only helping the poor by providing them quality education and better living conditions but also becoming their voice. I am a well-known activist who represents the people and helps them in solving any concerns they have, be it their education or employment. My efforts are aligned to help the people lead a better and dignified life.
