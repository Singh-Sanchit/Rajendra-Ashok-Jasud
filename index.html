My Struggle, My Start

I, Rajendra Ashok Jasood, who was educated till Tenth and worked as a waiter in Tata Motors, then established my own business in Valathor in 20 years. I suggests to the young people that if any sincere efforts are made, then any business can succeed, so try-hard. I has also decided to set up hospitals and colleges for the poor with a view to social awareness. I was born and raised in the countryside, wanted to get higher education but started a waiter job to take care of my sisters and brothers.

I read examples of how many professionals created the world from zero. Since then, I kept my dream of setting up an industry. One day I would have a company too, and I insisted that I too would grow up. But nowadays, people used to tingle, to hear if your eyes were affected, to look at their worthiness, before they could hear it. But the dream came true, so stubborn that their faith never diminished.

I started the business of milking house-to-house milk at a lower capital cost by examining alternatives suggested by friends. However, as it had the opportunity to grow to a limited extent, I continued to focus on the scrap market. Then got the job of building a company in Ranjangaon MIDC, it did not miss the opportunity, and later established two IT companies as Kharadi Knowledge Park and Clock Tower in Pune.

My Profession, My Expansion

Realizing that there was a huge opportunity for the scrap market abroad, I also stepped in and never looked back. Even though the downturn has affected the business right now, there is a good chance in this business. My company is one of the three licensed to break transformers in the Gulf. Now that my business is judged worldwide, my goal is to have warehouses and offices in most countries. In this business too, there are frauds, already taking the money and paying the goods differently, the party escapes and gives poor quality goods.

Now I have also learned how to avoid this deception. Education is the foundation of success, my education was low but self-confident and stubborn, so now with practice, I can handle all the latest tools and make big transactions. I regularly paid social service because I was aware that the debt would not fit, I had to give something to the community. Help for needy students and poor patients continues.

I work on setting up a free hospital for the poor and a college for the talented students was underway. I have found myself dealing with addiction as a family, so they strongly advise young people to stay away from addictions. Luck has contributed to our success, I worked honestly and stubbornly and luck continued to support me. Likewise, you should try so hard that your support will be destined, too.
